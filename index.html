1. Who we are

MovingMate is published by GungnirNet, an independent software developer (sole proprietor; Norwegian ENK registration planned). The app is listed on Google Play under « GungnirNet ». For data protection questions or to exercise your rights, contact movingmate@gungnirnet.eu. We act as data controller for personal data described below.

2. What we collect

  • Account: email address and authentication identifiers (Supabase Auth).
  • Inventory & moves: box/furniture descriptions, rooms, dimensions, values, QR identifiers, move names, invite codes, and data you choose to sync to the cloud.
  • Condition photos (optional, paid plans): JPEG images you capture to document item state (packed, pickup, delivery) for insurance or liability. Stored on your device; synced to Supabase Storage (EU) only with a paid plan and cloud sync enabled. Visible to move participants you invite. The camera is also used for QR scanning and AR measurement without saving photos for those features.
  • Device: app version, OS, and technical logs needed for support and security.
  • Voice & AI (optional): audio you submit for transcription or cloud AI, processed per your settings (on-device models stay on the device unless you use cloud AI).
  • Payments: transaction IDs and subscription status from Apple, Google, and RevenueCat — we do not store full card numbers.
  • Local data: inventory stored in on-device databases when you use offline mode.

3. Why we use your data (legal bases)

  • Provide the Service (contract — Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): account, sync, sharing, billing entitlements.
  • Security & fraud prevention (legitimate interests — Art. 6(1)(f)): logs, abuse detection.
  • Legal compliance (legal obligation — Art. 6(1)(c)): tax and accounting records for purchases.
  • Marketing emails only with your consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — not sent by default.

4. How we share data

We use trusted processors under data processing agreements:

  • Supabase (EU-hosted database, auth & object storage) — cloud inventory sync and condition photos.
  • Apple / Google / RevenueCat — in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Mistral AI and Google (Gemini API) — cloud AI when you use those features.
  • Move participants you invite — inventory data and condition photos for shared moves only.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. International transfers

We aim to process data in the EU/EEA (Supabase EU regions). Cloud AI requests may be processed by Mistral AI and/or Google outside the EEA; we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions where required.

6. Retention

  • Account & synced inventory: kept while your account is active; deleted or anonymized within 30 days after verified deletion request, except where law requires longer retention.
  • Synced condition photos: deleted with your account or move data; local copies remain on your device until you remove them or uninstall the app.
  • Billing records: retained as required by tax law (typically up to 10 years).
  • Support logs: up to 12 months unless needed for security investigations.

7. Your GDPR rights

If you are in the EU/EEA/UK, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object (where applicable), and port your data, and to withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing. Email movingmate@gungnirnet.eu — we respond within one month. You may lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority (e.g. Datatilsynet in Norway, or CNIL in France).

8. Security

We use encryption in transit (TLS), row-level security on cloud data, and industry-standard access controls. No method is 100% secure — keep your device and email account protected.

9. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us to request deletion if you believe a child provided personal data.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of material changes in the app and ask for renewed consent when required. Version 2026-07-07. Last updated: July 2026.

Si vous êtes dans l'UE/EEE/Royaume-Uni, vous disposez des droits d'accès, de rectification, d'effacement, de limitation, d'opposition (le cas échéant) et de portabilité, ainsi que du retrait du consentement sans affecter le traitement antérieur.
